About Me

Hey there! My name is Zeinab Fawaz, and I am an Arts student currently pursuing a career within the Communications field. I have 2+ years of experience in social media coordination and management derived from volunteer work.

I am an enthusiastic individual with a deep passion for social media and content creation. Currently, I volunteer as a social media coordinator for my community organization, where I plan and advertise social, religious, and educational events. My absolute favourite part about this role is capturing memories during events behind an iPhone and DSLR camera and later compiling them into engaging and meaningful content!
